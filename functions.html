Systems & Features

Discover the core mechanics and what each one brings to the game.

Inventory System

Stores items collected by players using arrays or a data table. Includes equipment slots, quick-access bar, sorting filters, and sync across multiplayer sessions.

Ability System & Skill Tree

Employ Unreal’s Gameplay Ability System (GAS) to handle abilities, attributes, cooldowns, and statuses. Create a skill tree that players unlock over time, connecting to character progression and the dragon’s growth.

Dragon Companion

Implement an AI-driven companion that hatches from an egg and evolves as the story progresses. Manage hunger, fear, and bonding mechanics. Integrate with the ability system for dragon attacks and defenses.

Co‑op Networking

Use Unreal’s built-in multiplayer and session management to allow drop-in/out co‑op. Replicate inventory, abilities, and quests. Implement voice chat and emotes for communication.

Missions & Quests

Create a mission manager that tracks objectives, progress, and rewards. Support branching dialogues and consequences. Integrate with save/load and UI so quests persist across sessions.

AI & Enemies

Design enemy behaviour trees, perception, combat AI, and pathfinding. Include different enemy types such as cultists, creatures of the ashlands, and mini‑bosses.

Save/Load System

Utilise Unreal’s SaveGame system to persist player progress, inventory, quests, and world state. Implement auto‑save checkpoints and manual saving.

UI & Interaction

Design intuitive menus, HUD, and dialogue boxes using UMG. Support gamepad and keyboard/mouse controls. Provide visual feedback for items, interactions, and abilities.

Open World & Streaming

Partition the world into streaming levels, load/unload zones seamlessly, and handle player respawn locations. Implement day/night cycles and environmental events.

Audio & VFX

Integrate a sound system (FMOD or Wwise), create ambient soundscapes, and design particle effects for spells, dragon fire, weather, and explosions.

Vehicles

Add drivable carts or vehicles for faster travel. Handle vehicle physics, collisions, and player interactions. Include upgradeable mounts later.

Weapon System

Create melee and ranged weapons with different stats, upgrades, and combos. Integrate with the ability system for special attacks and weapon abilities.

Crafting & Shelters

Allow players to collect resources and craft gear, potions, and shelters. Introduce a base‑building mechanic where they construct camps, upgrade structures, and manage resources.
