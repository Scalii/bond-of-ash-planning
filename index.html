Development Roadmap

Follow the journey from concept to finished game.

1

Core Setup

Establish the project structure, set up version control, and integrate Unreal Engine plugins (Online Subsystem, GAS, FMOD).

2

World Building

Design the open world layout, implement world partition and level streaming, and set up dynamic day/night and weather systems.

3

Core Systems

Implement the inventory, ability & skill tree, dragon companion, and networking. Focus on core mechanics with placeholders for art.

4

Gameplay Systems

Develop the mission/quest manager, AI and enemies, save/load functionality, and initial UI & interaction.

5

Advanced Features

Add drivable vehicles, weapon system, crafting and shelter construction. Polish the dragon companion’s growth and behaviours.

6

Polish & Optimisation

Integrate audio & VFX, optimise performance, fix bugs, and refine gameplay based on playtesting feedback.
